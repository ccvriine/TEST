Hello Kitty


Hello Kitty (Kitty White) is a female white
Japanese Bobtail Cat with a red bow worn on
her left ear and often wears blue overalls with a yellow shirt
on top created by Yuko Shimizu & Yuko Yamaguchi.
The character was first introduced in 1974 and appears in
various shows such as 'Hello Kitty and Friends,' Hello Kitty
is one of the most popular trademarks in the world.
My Melody


My Melody is a white rabbit from Sanrio.
Known as Hello Kitty's best friend, My Melody
is based on the 'Red Riding Hood,' which is why she
always appears with her iconic little pink hood
with a flower on the left side. She was introduced
in 1975 and was created by Yasuko Matsumoto. She comes
from Mariland and her favorite food is almond pound cake.
Kuromi


A white rabbit with a jester-like black hat with a
skull who changes to reflect her mood and who
considers herself My Melody's rival, Kuromi was
created by Junko Takeuchi in 2005. Born on
Halloween, Kuromi's name translates to 'chrome'
and she is the leader of the biker gang Kuromi 5.





Pompompurin


Pompompurin is a good-natured Golden Retriever
born on April 16th with the name Mr. Pudding, although it
was later changed. Pompompurin lives in his own basket
in the entrance hall of his sister-owner's house.
His trademark is his brown beret that is always on top
of his head. He was created in 1996 in a Sanrio
in-house contest.





Cinnamoroll


Cinamoroll is a chubby male dog who was born in
a high up cloud in the sky by Miyuki Okumura in
2001 by Sanrio. Cinamoroll made his debut in the
manga Fluffy, Fluffy Cinnamoroll. He is a little chubby
male dog with white fur, blue eyes, pink cheeks, a
plump and curly tail that resembles a cinnamon
roll, and long ears that enable him to fly




Chococat


Chococat is a Sanrio character introduced
in 1996, based on a little boy black cat.
His name is taken from his chocolate coloured
nose. He has no visible mouth,
like depictions of Hello Kitty.





Keroppi


Keroppi is a green little frog created by Sanrio.
With green skin and a V-shaped mouth, Keroppi is a
happy little frog who lives in Donut Pond and
enjoys cooking. He lives with Keroma, Keroppa, Pikki,
Koroppi, Kerobah, Kerojee, and Chippi. He was created by
Tomoe Iwata in 1988.




Pochacco


Pochacco is a white dog with no visible mouth who was born
in Nightingale, Brazil. He now lives in Fuwa Fuwa City. Known as
the 'Yorimichi Dog,' Pochacco loves soccer,
basketball, and skateboarding. Pochacco was born on
February 29th; his favorite food is banana ice cream.
He was created by Minoru Onoue in 1989.





Tuxedo Sam


Tuxedo Sam, also known as Tuxedo J. Orville Samuel,
is a male penguin from Antarctica,
although he resides in London, England.
A clumsy little gentleman with an unbeatable fashion sense,
he owns 365 bow ties and speaks excellent
english. He was created in 1979 by Yumiko Chiba.
